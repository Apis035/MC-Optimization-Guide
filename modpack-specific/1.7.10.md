## Project Ozone 2

1. Download the latest version of Project Ozone 2
2. Remove FastCraft
3. Replace NotEnoughItems, LogisticsPipes Chisel, ForgeMultipart, CodeChickenCore and CodeChickenLib with their Unofficial (GTNH) versions. Some of them are on CurseForge, but some can only be downloaded from GitHub.
4. Install [ModularUI 2](https://github.com/GTNewHorizons/ModularUI)
5. Install [these mods](https://github.com/Radk6/MC-Optimization-Guide/blob/main/mods-n-stuff/1.7.10.md)

Known issues: None (yet?)

Note: You may need to replace a few other mods (Carpenters' Blocks? Tinkers' Construct?) with their GTNH versions.

3. Replace NotEnoughItems, Chisel, Wireless Redstone-CBE, ForgeMultipart, CodeChickenCore and CodeChickenLib with their Unofficial (GTNH) versions. Some of them are on CurseForge, but some can only be downloaded from GitHub.
4. Install [these mods](https://github.com/Radk6/MC-Optimization-Guide/blob/main/mods-n-stuff/1.7.10.md)

Known issues: None (yet?)

Note: You may need to replace a few other mods (Carpenters' Blocks? Tinkers' Construct?) with their GTNH versions.
